*("The Tavern" — where every night in BR is an open mic)*

**Born:** Baton Rouge, Louisiana (Southside)  
**Age:** 23  
**Label:** Independent / Tiger City Records  

---

## THE VISION

Tiger City Tavern is BR's wild card — a rapper who walks into a room and turns it into a full blown experience. He represents the gritty side of Baton Rouge but carries himself like he just stepped off a tour bus in Houston or ATL. He pays homage to his city the way Drake pays homage to Toronto — unapologetically, boldly, everywhere he goes.

**The Sound:**  
Think Baton Rouge chopped-and-screwed roots meets Houston zydeco-techno fusion meets grungy Trapwire-era rap meets Atlanta club swagger. Live shows feel like a party that might spiral into a riot. No cap.

**The Aesthetic:**  
- Texas swag: oversized, flashy, confident  
- Louisiana grit: from the swamp, don't flinch  
- Wayne energy: smooth, effortless, generational talent vibes  
- Drake DNA: melodic hooks, emotional depth, crossover appeal  
- BR pride: LSU purple and gold, Tiger Stadium energy  

**The Hook:**  
When Tiger City Tavern raps about Baton Rouge, the world knows it. He's not trying to be from somewhere else — he's making BR the place everyone wants to be from.
